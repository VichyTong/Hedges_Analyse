World’s Fastest Men Will Face Off Again—Finally—in Italy
Italian Lamont Marcell Jacobs and American Fred Kerley haven’t raced each other since Jacobs’s surprise 100-meter win at the Tokyo Olympics in 2021.
The two fastest men in the world have been engaging in a tart verbal rematch of their 2021 Tokyo Olympics showdown on social media in recent weeks. Now, the race is actually going to happen in real life—and one man will have a home-field advantage.
